Limited Liability Company

Limited Liability Company is the most common option in which the entity must have one or more Qatari partner whose share capital must not be less than 51%. AYAM QATAR can arrange you professional Qatari nationals as your local partner. There are many key advantages to setting up an LLC

1. No limitation on the amount of products or services the company can sell

2. Your company will be recognized as a local company, often permitting the entity to take part in an increased number of government and private tenders

3. The company will not be subject to withholding tax

Ayam Qatar understand that finding a transparent and trustworthy partner is the most critical step for your businesses.Our experts typically examine each client’s corporate requirement and advice on the most appropriate structure of incorporation.
